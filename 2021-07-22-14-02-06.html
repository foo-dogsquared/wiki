Learning techniques

Date: 2021-07-22 14:02:06 +08:00Date modified: 2021-07-28 11:17:06 +08:00

    • Spaced repetition and Recalling are two fundamental practice for learning.

    • Deliberate practice helps in increasing our skill level and a necessity if we want to really master the concepts.

    • Actively Switching between different topics makes new perspective while preventing Tunnel vision.

    • Communicate with others to learn new things include their way of understanding a topic. Follow the experts in the field.

    • Skimming the material helps being familiar with the topic. Speedreading is all about skimming well.
