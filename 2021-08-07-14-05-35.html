Balancing creativity and familiarity

Date: 2021-08-07 14:05:35 +08:00Date modified: 2021-08-07 14:06:29 +08:00

    • a familiar idea has more value compared to more alien ideas

    • the most satisfying act for creativity is balancing the new idea with familiarity

    • this practice can be stretched into Deliberate practice, seeing creativity more of a skill than an inherent talent

    • remember, Creativity does not mean originality
