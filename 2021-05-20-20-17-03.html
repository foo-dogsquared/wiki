Don't get caught in the hype

Date: 2021-05-20 20:17:03 +08:00Date modified: 2021-06-18 20:28:30 +08:00

    • a simple advice with a simple reason

    • oftentimes, when a thing caught mainstream attention, it mostly dives into Professional narcissism on the thing

    • e.g., how this will make you 150% productive, up to 200k members have already registered with this tool

    • not much focus on the what and the why

    • when Roam Research caught up, most focus on the attention of how this make your note-taking improve

    • that said, we do sometimes get caught in it and Involuntary attention switch is good for preventing tunnel vision
