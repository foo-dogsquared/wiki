How To Draw Anything

Date: 2021-09-19 20:54:51 +08:00Date modified: 2022-01-07 11:22:20 +08:00

    • "to draw" means "to pull"; pulling the images into lines, gradually improving the image

    • even the masters of art struggles conjuring an image first try; this is where sketching comes in handy

    • sketching is the ability to quickly pull the image

    • the drawing process consists of creating loose lines, slowly improving the lines, then finalizing the image when you're done

    • practically, the very basis of sketching is starting with the simplest basis and gradually adding details; in the demo, the sketch starts with the basic shapes — e.g., two circles for the jar and a circle for the apple; furthermore, the simplified foundation should also observe the relationships as we pull the image to our drawing — e.g., the verticals of the image, the distance and/or proportion;
